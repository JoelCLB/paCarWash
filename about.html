About Us

Chinhoyi residents are you looking for a place to chillout and have a meal? We got just the place for you. Pa CarWash Restaurant is a new restaurant in town where food is as fresh as the wash! Pa CarWash is the perfect spot for anyone looking to enjoy a yummy meal in Chinhoyi. We offer a wide variety of affordable and healthy dishes that cater for everyone. We also offer student discounts and daily specials for CUT student to help you save even more. So come on in, relax, and enjoy a delicious meal with us!
